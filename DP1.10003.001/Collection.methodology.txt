Point counts at all sites are conducted by local contracted experts. At larger sites, point counts occur once per breeding season at 5-15 nine point grids with the grid centers colocated with the center of Distributed Base Plots. At smaller sites (cannot accommodate the minimum of 5 grids) point counts will be conducted from the southwest corner (point 21) of 5-25 Distributed Base Plots, twice per breeding season. All point counts are six minutes long.
